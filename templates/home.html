Welcome to Pickup!


Search for all your local pickup games! Pickup provides the ability to search for your favorite local sports games and join on any pickup games happening in your area right now! Just pick your preffered location and your sport, and find out the local games that are playing around you!

Start your own pickup games

Have an urge to play soccer right now? Pickup lets you start your own pickup games at your local favorite parks. Simply make an event, choose a location and a time, and people will flock to play your sport right away!

Search for your favorite games

Don't want to make a game? Search from millions of games currently going on right now from our database! Simply see what's going on in your area and go enjoy playing and meeting your new friends.

Recieve custom notifications

Want to get notified if there are relavent games around you? Pickup notifies you automatically to see what games are happening in your area that you would be interested in. You can join these events right away and meet your new teammates on the field right away.

Edit your profile at will

As you grow more experienced, your skills change. Edit your profile to update your reflective max skills so that cames are more custom tailored to your needs.
